Mercedes-Benz S560

On the other hand, the Mercedes-Benz S560 features a 4.0-litre V8 engine that produces 463 horsepower. The S560’s suspension system is also designed to provide a smooth and comfortable ride, making it a strong contender in the performance category.

Technological Advancements
