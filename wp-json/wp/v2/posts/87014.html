[vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce the launch of its “Stage III BBK” High-Performance Braking System specifically designed, optimized and manufactured for use on modern civilian armored vehicles. Built upon decades of precision-engineering expertise, Manorluxx\u00ae has developed an industry-first, heavy-duty platform for unmatched responsiveness in critical stopping maneuvers in even the most challenging situations. At present, this is the only high-performance braking system in the world that was designed and engineered by an armored vehicle manufacturer in-house, and available for a wide range of modern vehicles.<\/p>\n

\u201cManorluxx\u00ae strives to remain at the forefront of the armored vehicle industry through its continuous innovations in advanced security features and vehicle performance,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Co-Founder and Chairwoman of Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. \u201cArmored vehicles have become a critical security asset for governments, VIPs, and private firms \u2013 however, the added weight of armoring has always posed a significant challenge in the industry. Today, our team is excited to introduce our in-house solution as a revolutionary standalone brake system.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

The Manorluxx\u00ae High-Performance Brake Kit features advanced braking technology and an innovative design that is specifically engineered to counterbalance the added weight of the vehicle\u2019s armoring by increasing the braking capabilities to an impressive rating (GVWR) of up to 6,400kg, exceeding the stringent requirements of the US and EU automotive safety standards. With high-performance calipers, larger slotted rotors, and racing-grade ceramic brake pads that cover a larger surface area \u2013 the Manorluxx\u00ae braking system optimizes braking performance to ensure shorter stopping distances while providing better control in the event of a sudden stop in the face of obstacles or danger.<\/p>\n

Using the highest quality materials and state-of-the-art machining techniques, the Manorluxx\u00ae brake system delivers exceptional durability and reliability that meets the unique demands of armored vehicles. The streamlined design and improved ventilation technology also provide rapid heat dissipation and advanced cooling to withstand the high temperatures caused from absorbing the force of the vehicle\u2019s excessive weight, significantly reducing the early wear of the brake pads which could compromise safety.
