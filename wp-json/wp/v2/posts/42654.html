INKAS\u00ae Aerospace & Defense is proud to announce that it will be exhibiting at the 2022 ISS World expo in Dubai \u2013 one of the largest events for regional law enforcement, intelligence, and homeland security analysts. ISS World presents the prominent techniques and solutions used by police forces, public safety organizations, government, and private sector communities in the fight against drug and human trafficking, money laundering, terrorism, and other criminal activities predominant in today\u2019s digital landscape.<\/p>\n

\u201cINKAS strives for quality, innovation, security, and performance in every solution we deliver,\u201d said Eugene Gerstein, Managing Partner at INKAS\u00ae Aerospace & Defense. \u201cWe look forward to attending the ISS World expo which features seminars led by distinguished law enforcement officers and Ph.D. scientists to discuss the state of the industry and create partnerships for a more secure future.\u201d<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae Aerospace & Defense is the newest division of INKAS\u00ae Group of Companies, as part of its mission to develop comprehensive solutions to solve the ever-changing security challenges of today in order to create a safer tomorrow. Led by a team of former defense and law enforcement professionals, INKAS progressive products provide advanced, field-proven solutions for intelligence and physical security.<\/p>\n

INKAS delivers extensive expertise in addressing the ever-evolving threats to defense and security \u2013 having established a vast network of best-in-class services across 60+ countries to deliver unparalleled knowledge and cutting-edge solutions that meet the unique requirements of each client. With a technology-first corporate philosophy, the company delivers on its mission to provide forward-thinking innovations, when and where they are needed.<\/p>\n

From intelligence solutions to physical security, INKAS\u00ae Aerospace & Defense is a globally leading manufacturer and integrator of intelligence and defense technology. Developing comprehensive, state-of-the-art security products, INKAS delivers end-to-end solutions for governments, law enforcement, port security, rapid response teams, and corporations alike. From protecting public safety, to facilitating intelligence-based law enforcement \u2013 INKAS supports the mission-critical operations that matter most, and delivers top-tier products and solutions on a global scale.<\/p>\n
