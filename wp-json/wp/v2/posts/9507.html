Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> Armored Vehicle Manufacturing has been named as the Best International Business at the 2017 Chinese American Entrepreneur Awards held by the Association of Chinese American Entrepreneurs (ACCE). It is a very special trophy for the Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> team as it is the first time in the history of the ACCE that a non-Chinese company has won the award.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe would like to express our sincere gratitude to the Association of Chinese American Entrepreneurs for this prestigious recognition,\u201d said David Khazanski, CEO, Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. \u201cAs an export-oriented business, we look forward to building and developing strong bilateral relations between China and Canada in order to extend our business in Asia and bring more business and trade contracts to Ontario\u201d.<\/p>\n

Since 1994, ACCE has been one of the largest business associations in Canada and significantly contributes to trade relationships between China and Canada.
