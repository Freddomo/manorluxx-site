Over two decades of proven performance, INKAS\u00ae\u00a0Armored Vehicle Manufacturing<\/a> has become one of the largest armored vehicle manufacturers in the world \u2013 delivering high quality protective vehicles for private, commercial and law enforcement purposes worldwide. As a global leader in its space, INKAS\u00ae is proud to announce its appearance at the 21st<\/sup>\u00a0session of the Leading Homeland Security and Safety event \u2013 Milipol Paris 2019 on November 19-22.<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae invites everyone to join the company for its opening keynote ceremony at 12:00 pm at their stand (5 D 076). Drinks and light snacks would be served.<\/p>\n

At Milipol 2019, INKAS\u00ae will display a lineup of multipurpose armored vehicles, including personnel carriers and tactical special purpose vehicles. Apart from that INKAS\u00ae will showcase its unique vehicle-mounted signal jamming system and other tactical applications.\u00a0\u201cWe have an international reputation for producing top quality and dependable defensive vehicles that protect personnel in law enforcement, peacekeeping missions, border control agencies, as well as high-risk civilians such as diplomats, political leaders, and dignitaries,\u201d says Philip Daskal, Vice President of Sales, INKAS\u00ae. \u201cMaking use of our 100% in-house design, fabrication and assembling capacities, INKAS\u00ae consistently offers the fastest vehicle turnaround times in the market.\u201d<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae welcomes all interested parties to its booth at Milipol (Stand 5 D 076) to discover its new generation of vehicles and integrated defensive technologies. Milipol Paris 2019 will take place from Tuesday November 19th<\/sup> to Friday, November 22nd <\/sup>2019, 9:00am \u2013 5:00pm at the Paris-Nord Villepinte Exhibition Centre.<\/p>\n
