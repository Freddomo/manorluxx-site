The Ontario Chamber of Commerce (OCC) announced that INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing, a Canadian-based leader in the fabrication of armored vehicles, has been named the winner of the\u00a0Beyond Borders, Medium Business Award\u00a0at the 2018 Ontario Business Achievement Awards (OBAAs). This award recognizes\u00a0a medium-sized business that has implemented an export strategy that has positively affected its bottom line.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe are truly humbled to be recognized with an Ontario Business Achievement Award as it endorses our export growth and celebrates our commitment to excellence,\u201d says Philip Daskal, VP of International Sales at INKAS\u00ae. \u201cWe strive to continuously provide the best products in the industry with an individual approach to each client. We are privileged to operate in the province of Ontario that consistently delivers highly-skilled labour with diversified experience \u2013 which are both important factors for any export-oriented business.<\/p>\n

Hailed as the most recognized industry gala in the province with a single focus on celebrating business success, the\u00a0OBAAs have recognized the incredible innovations and achievements of Ontario businesses since 1983. This is the second time in award\u2019s history when the prestigious Beyond Borders, Medium Business Award, goes to INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing.<\/p>\n

\u201cHundreds of business and community leaders from across Ontario came out to celebrate the achievements of each of the 2018\u00a0OBAA\u00a0finalists.\u00a0INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing\u00a0has set the standard for innovation and creativity,\u201d said\u00a0Rocco\u00a0Rossi, President and CEO of the Ontario Chamber of Commerce. \u201cThis\u00a0Beyond Borders, Medium Business Award\u00a0is a testament to the skill, integrity, and hard work of\u00a0INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Ontario Business Achievement Awards (OBAAs) celebrates organizations and individuals whose dedication and accomplishments have made them industry leaders in the province. Ontario Chamber of Commerce (OCC) has been the independent, non-partisan voice of Ontario business. The OCC\u2019s mission is to support economic growth in Ontario by defending business priorities at Queen\u2019s Park on behalf of its network\u2019s diverse 60,000 members.
