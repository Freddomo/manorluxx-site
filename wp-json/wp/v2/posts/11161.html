INKAS\u00ae<\/sup> Armored Vehicle Manufacturing has successfully reengineered it\u2019s popular Sentry APC (Armoured Personnel Carrier) to now offer a Right-Hand Drive (RHD) version of its field-proven armored vehicle. The INKAS\u00ae Sentry APC is a tactical vehicle designed to serve in extreme climate conditions and harsh terrains. It is a unique APC in terms of technical specifications, design and assembly and is one of the only right-hand drive conversions in the world to be carried out 100% in-house, with the same company both designing and armoring the vehicle.<\/p>\n

The heavy-duty Ford\u00ae<\/sup> chassis that powers the Sentry APC is one of the most preferred and widely used platforms within mid-scale APCs and LAPVs with 8-to-10 person capacities. The Ford\u00ae chassis offers a reliable and easy-to-maintain skeleton upon which to build out the APC. While there is a large need for these types of vehicles within volatile regions, approximately one third of the world\u2019s population is left unserved because a Ford\u00ae RHD chassis is not commercially available.<\/p>\n

As a result, INKAS\u00ae<\/sup> tasked its world-class engineering division with developing a right-hand-drive conversion for the Sentry APC to meet and exceed OEM quality and performance characteristics.<\/p>\n

This was a major challenge and required significant redesign to relocate and further reinforce the vehicle\u2019s wiring, drivetrain components, steering and exhaust systems \u2013 however, INKAS\u00ae was successful at manufacturing a 100% road-ready right-hand-drive chassis to accommodate the armoring process.<\/p>\n
