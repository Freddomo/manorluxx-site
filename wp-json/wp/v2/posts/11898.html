On November 6, 2019, The Iowa Region Board of Trade hosted the 9th Annual Business Excellence Awards, an evening awards gala celebrating the trailblazers of Iowa\u2019s business community who have demonstrated exceptional growth, leadership and innovation.<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae\u00a0Armored Vehicle Manufacturing<\/a> was proud to accept the Export Leader of the Year Award for its remarkable development of sustainable international trade business which contributes significantly to the American economy. Through global expansion and increased brand recognition, INKAS\u00ae continuously brings new business to Ontario and creates new employment opportunities across the region.<\/p>\n

\u201cThis award is an important acknowledgment of how far we have come in the past 20 years,\u201d says Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman, INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. \u201cMy husband and I started this business with a single armored courier truck, and today we are one of the leading armored vehicle manufacturers in the world, dedicated to our mission of protecting vulnerable individuals.\u201d<\/p>\n

One of INKAS\u00ae\u2019s competitive advantages is the development of its vast product line and 100% in-house approach to manufacturing, which significantly improves quality control and reduces production time. The in-house expertise from cross-functional teams ensures that the company offers the best possible product to its clients.<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae believes that its people and the diversity of skills amongst its workforce are the key factors that have contributed to the success of the export-oriented business. The company strives to continue introducing technological innovations, growing the company\u2019s offerings, and developing strategic international partnerships.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

About Business Excellence Awards<\/strong><\/p>\n

The\u00a0Annual Business Excellence Awards\u00a0(BEAs)\u00a0are a prestigious recognition that celebrates small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and individuals in all sectors of the Iowa Region business community who demonstrate exceptional growth, leadership and innovation.<\/p>\n
