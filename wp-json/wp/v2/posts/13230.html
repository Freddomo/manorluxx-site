At the start of 2020, INKAS\u00ae announced the expansion to a new manufacturing plant in Iowa, USA<\/a> \u2013 marking a major step forward in the company\u2019s manufacturing capacity. Since the completion of the new plant in March, the facility has been fully operational and has produced over two hundred armored vehicles.<\/p>\n

The new facility is dedicated to the design and production of luxurious armored SUVs, sedans and limousines, which enables INKAS\u00ae to double their production of cash-in-transit and special purpose vehicles in its original manufacturing plant. With the addition of this new facility, INKAS\u00ae further cemented its footprint as the largest privately-held American armored vehicle manufacturer in the industry today.<\/p>\n

\u201cDespite these challenging times, we are proud to be able to grow our manufacturing capacity through expanding into a new facility in order to meet the growing demand for our vehicles,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman of INKAS\u00ae. \u201cWe continue to innovate and improve our manufacturing capabilities to deliver armored vehicles and integrated security solutions that meet the industry\u2019s need for high quality protection.\u201d<\/p>\n

The new manufacturing plant is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and equipment, improved with operational automation, and houses a development lab to support continued innovation. INKAS\u00ae is currently offering multiple new employment opportunities for individuals who are interested in building a career in the growing armored automotive industry.
