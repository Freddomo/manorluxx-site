Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is pleased to announce it will be exhibiting in the 2023 International Armoured Vehicle Expo \u2013 showcasing its cutting-edge armored vehicles and comprehensive defense solutions. As the world\u2019s largest armored vehicle expo, the event will bring together 750+ experts from the army and defense communities to highlight the latest developments and emerging innovations that are critical to the future of the armored vehicle industry.<\/p>\n

\u201cAs a forwarding thinking company, Manorluxx\u00ae constantly seeks to improve its proprietary armoring systems and create new security solutions that will stand up to the threats of tomorrow,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman and Co-Founder of Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. \u201cManorluxx\u00ae strives to stay ahead-of-the-curve with the latest advancements in technology in order to continuously enhance our extensive lineup of best-in-class armored vehicles. Amongst Manorluxx\u00ae industry-leading solutions is its VPAM VR7 certified Armored Toyota Land Cruiser 300 \u2013 a ballistic certification that puts Manorluxx in the top 1% of manufacturers who are capable of achieving such a high level of precision engineering. The VPAM certification exemplifies Manorluxx\u00ae unparalleled expertise, meticulous engineering, and exacting skill and technique that this certification demands. The remarkable workmanship of Manorluxx\u00ae world-class engineers is further showcased by the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry APC \u2013 an innovative multipurpose vehicle designed for exceptional off-road performance on even the most challenging terrains and most extreme climates.<\/p>\n

The Manorluxx\u00ae team will be highlighting its Special Purpose Vehicle lineup at the IAV expo this month, as part of the armored vehicle community\u2019s latest developments. To learn more about Manorluxx\u00ae innovative armored vehicles and comprehensive defense solutions, visit the Manorluxx team at Stand B5<\/strong> at the 2023 International Armoured Vehicle Expo, in London\u2019s Twickenham Stadium from January 23rd to 26th<\/sup>.<\/p>\n
