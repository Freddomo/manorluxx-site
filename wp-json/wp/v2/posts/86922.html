[vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce a strategically important collaboration with Force Development Services (FDS) \u2013 fusing the industry\u2019s most reliable heavy-capacity tactical intervention system with Manorluxx\u00ae globally-renowned armored vehicles to create a lineup of highly capable Armored Tactical Intervention Vehicles. This collaboration will produce vehicle-mounted and rapidly deployable intervention capability that delivers unparalleled performance in mission-critical operations across a multitude of scenarios.<\/p>\n

\u201cAs global leaders in our respective fields, we are excited to team up to develop innovative armored tactical intervention vehicles,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman and Co-Founder of Manorluxx\u00ae. \u201cThe FDS tactical intervention systems are an excellent fit within the Manorluxx\u00ae special purpose vehicle lineup and will enhance the capabilities of the tactical teams and special forces that have entrusted us for over two decades.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

As a complete system that can be deployed and operated directly from the vehicle, Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Tactical Intervention Vehicles eliminate the need for security forces to await secondary support vehicles prior to acting, this enables tactical teams to respond significantly faster in time-sensitive situations that involve hostages, criminals, terrorists, or active emergencies where lives may be at risk.<\/p>\n

Claire Hemmings, CEO of Force Development Services said. \u201cWe are absolutely delighted that the collaboration has been such a success. Working closely together, the teams have created a very special capability that will equip users with a vital competitive advantage\u201d.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

Designed specifically for Counter Terror teams, the FDS Vehicle Borne Intervention Systems have been operationally proven for over two decades and are known to enable users to achieve successful outcomes especially in extremely demanding scenarios.
