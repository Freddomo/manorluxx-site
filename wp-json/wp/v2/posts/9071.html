Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> Group of Companies, a world-leading security solutions provider, has signed a multi-million agreement with ISP, LLC for research and development (R&D) and production of special purpose vehicles, safes and other integrated security products, systems and solutions. The project will be jointly executed by Mayotex-Philcar, which has been recently acquired by Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup>, and Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> Armored Vehicle Manufacturing, a leading division of the Group in Canada.<\/p>\n

The agreement has been signed as a part of the Ontario Business Mission to Israel led by Kathleen Wynne, the Premier of Ontario. The mission\u2019s goal was to further establish Ontario as a top innovation and knowledge economy partner and develop the strong economic and cultural ties between Ontario and Israel by creating new commercial partnerships leading to economic growth and job creation.<\/p>\n

Israel is a priority market for Ontario for trade and research. Ontario and Israel have had a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) in place for over a decade to promote industrial research and development collaborations. The agreements signed during the mission are expected to create about 200 jobs within Ontario.<\/p>\n

\u201cOntario and Israel have a long history of working together on research and development initiatives,\u201d said Premier Wynne. \u201cThis mission has created opportunities for us to strengthen these partnerships and create new ones. The agreement signed between Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup> and ISP will strengthen Ontario\u2019s reputation as a world leader in the security field and create jobs in both Ontario and Israel.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cWe are delighted to execute this large scale project for R&D and production of security solutions in partnership with\u00a0ISP, LLC and benefit both Canadian and Israeli economies,\u201d said David Khazanski, Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup>\u00a0Group of Companies\u00a0President and CEO. \u201cOur advanced technological security solutions, production capacities and international presence provide us with clear competitive advantages to fulfil the needs of our global clientele.\u201d<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
