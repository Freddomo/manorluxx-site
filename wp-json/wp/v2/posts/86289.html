Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce it will be exhibiting at this year\u2019s Aidex tradeshow \u2013 featuring Manorluxx\u00ae field-proven armored vehicles that have been deployed in humanitarian aid and disaster relief missions around the globe for over 25 years to protect the teams who put their lives at risk in order bring stability and assistance to devasted regions across the globe.<\/p>\n

\u201cAs a forward-thinking company, Manorluxx\u00ae is driven by its vision of creating a better and more secure future, and safeguarding the lives of individuals and communities on a global scale,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Co-Founder and Chairwoman of Manorluxx\u00ae. \u201cWe are humbled that organizations such as the United Nations and NATO, as well as governments worldwide, continue to entrust our vehicles in the mission-critical operations that put their teams in direct danger in order to aid and assist greater populations.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

With extensive experience engineering vehicles for a wide range of critical field operations, Manorluxx\u00ae unparalleled level of expertise is reflected in its innovative armored vehicles such as the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry \u2013 one of the most versatile armored vehicles on the market, that can be customized to fit any mission type. Designed as a heavy-duty, multi-terrain armored vehicle for extreme climate conditions, the Sentry is available in a multitude of configurations such as an armored personnel carrier and a medical evacuation vehicle, and is fully customizable to meet the specific needs of each mission and client.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe impact that NGOs have in establishing peace and security in regions around the globe is vital to so many communities worldwide, and Manorluxx\u00ae continuously strives to push the boundaries of its industry to deliver innovative armored vehicles that ensure the safe transportation of aid and relief teams through volatile regions and that protect these individuals from the life-threatening scenarios that they face,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma.<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

To learn more about Manorluxx\u00ae advanced security features and mission-specific capabilities, visit the Manorluxx\u00ae team at stand B51 at the 2022 Aidex event in Brussels, Belgium on November 16th and 17th.<\/p>\n

To learn more about the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry, please visit the vehicle page here<\/a>.<\/p>\n
