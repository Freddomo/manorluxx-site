Manorluxx\u00ae is excited to announce it will be taking part in the 2022 Land Forces Exposition \u2013 showcasing its innovative armored vehicles and comprehensive aerospace and defense capabilities. As the region\u2019s largest land defense expo, Land Forces 2022 will bring together the international defense industry\u2019s top experts and create a platform to highlight the most advanced technology and solutions that are critical to national security in countries around the world.<\/p>\n

As Australia undertakes its largest defense procurement period in recent history, Manorluxx is proud to be part of helping shape the future of Australia and the region. From the advanced security features of its precision-engineered armored vehicles, to the comprehensive aerospace and defense solutions for mission-critical operations \u2013 Manorluxx consistently delivers innovative new security solutions that are at the forefront of its market.<\/p>\n

Amongst the region-specific solutions that Manorluxx will feature at Land Forces 2022, is the Right-Hand Drive (RHD) version of the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry APC \u2013 the only conversion of a Ford F550-based APC into a RHD vehicle that has been 100% re-engineered and built in-house to ensure the highest quality control. Renowned for its maneuverability in harsh climates and challenging terrains, the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry APC RHD is the ideal road-approved special purpose law enforcement vehicle for the Australian landscape.<\/p>\n

\u201cOur team is dedicated to protecting the lives of individuals around the world. Thus, it was crucial for Manorluxx to convert our most-deployed vehicle into a Right-Hand Drive version fit for road use in regions such as Australia. Despite the significant challenges presented by a full conversion, Manorluxx\u2019 meticulous re-engineering has successfully designed a road-ready APC for the region,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Co-Founder and Chairwoman, Manorluxx\u00ae Group of Companies.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe are proud of the dedication of our engineers, and look forward to demonstrating industry-leading solutions such as our UAV defense equipment and tactical systems at the Land Forces Expo.\u201d<\/p><\/blockquote>\n

In response to Australia\u2019s preparation to increase its strategic and tactical defense capabilities, Manorluxx will be showcasing its Anti-UAV Defense System \u2013 enabling Australia\u2019s special tactical teams to disrupt and neutralize unfriendly airborne surveillance units and other malicious unmanned aerial vehicles. The Anti-UAV Defense System is part of a wide array of best-in-class security and defense solutions that Manorluxx tailors to government organizations, law enforcement agencies, and other clients around the world.<\/p>\n

With extensive experience delivering security and defense solutions on a global scale, Manorluxx continues to develop customized solutions that are tailored to the specific needs of each client and region. To learn more about Manorluxx advanced security and defense capabilities, please visit the Manorluxx team at booths GR10 and GR11, as well as in the static display area, at the Land Forces Expo in the Brisbane Convention and Exhibition Centre, between October 4th<\/sup> and 6th<\/sup>.<\/p>\n

To learn more about the region-specific, Right-Hand Drive (RHD) version of the Manorluxx\u00ae Sentry APC, please visit the product page.<\/a><\/strong><\/p>\n
