Iowa, ON – American Business<\/em>\u00a0and\u00a0Maclean\u2019s<\/em>\u00a0rank INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing amongst Canada\u2019s Fastest-Growing Companies on its annual Growth 500 list. Despite already being one of the largest armored vehicle<\/a> manufacturers in the world for over two decades, the continuous innovation in INKAS\u00ae products<\/a> has led to more than doubling their revenue within the last five years.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe are honoured to be recognized on the Growth 500 ranking,\u201d says Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman, INKAS\u00ae Group of Companies. \u201cINKAS\u00ae has been successfully deploying numerous new technological and engineering advancements on a global scale for decades in order to protect lives in threatening situations. This recognition is an acknowledgment of the trust placed in us by those who find themselves in INKAS\u00ae armored vehicles.\u201d<\/p>\n

\u201cThe companies on the 2019 Growth 500 are truly remarkable. Demonstrating foresight, innovation, and smart management, their stories serve as a primer for how to build a successful entrepreneurial business today,\u201d says Beth Fraser, Growth 500 program manager. \u201cAs we celebrate over 30 years of Canada\u2019s Fastest-Growing Companies program, it\u2019s encouraging to see that entrepreneurship is healthier than ever in this country.\u201d<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae<\/a> constantly seeks out innovation and is challenging what is possible with lightweight armoring and defensive technologies. Safeguarding lives is our priority, and our products are manufactured to the highest quality in order to provide reliable protection to the clients that we serve. In order for the company to continue to grow, we maintain a deep understanding of our clients\u2019 changing needs \u2013 whether peacekeeping missions or personal protection. With nearly 20 years of industry experience, INKAS\u00ae is a true leader in the field, providing top-of-the-line armored vehicles for banks, law enforcement, corporations, and individuals on a global scale.<\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae continuously expands to new sectors and markets as well as geographies. The company invests a significant amount of resources into the R&D of new manufacturing processes, industry-leading technologies, and light-weight armoring composites ensuring that it always stays a few steps ahead of the competition while offering the best possible products.<\/p>\n
