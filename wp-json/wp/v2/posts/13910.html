INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce expedited procurement of its vehicles for the United States government. INKAS\u00ae has increased its capability and capacity to deliver high-quality armored vehicles and fleets, which are now available through the U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) database via their GSA Advantage program.<\/p>\n

Renowned for its world-class engineering expertise and state-of-the-art machinery, INKAS\u00ae has a proven track record in delivering exceptional products that meet and exceed OEM quality and performance, while delivering innovative defensive technology and high-quality armoring that meets CEN 1063 ballistic standards. INKAS\u00ae strict standards, combined with its proprietary ballistic armoring, is proven to provide 360-degree defense from attacks \u2013 delivering reliable protection when it matters the most.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe vehicles we manufacture play a crucial role in protecting the lives of government officials, diplomats, law enforcement agencies, executives, and high net worth individuals,\u201d said CEO David Khazanski. \u201cOur vehicles are engineered with attention to detail to provide unparalleled protection.\u201d<\/p>\n

Availability through the GSA database enables INKAS\u00ae to expedite orders to its United States government clientele, as well as expands the availability of industry-leading protective vehicles to all levels of the US government. GSA minimizes the red tape that typically delays contracts, and thus, significantly speeds up the procurement process for federal, state, and local governments, as well as other public entities.<\/p>\n

This expedited availability comes just in time as INKAS\u00ae second facility \u2013 which was acquired last year \u2013 has enabled the company to double its production capacity within the past year. This increased capacity, along with state-of-the-art solutions that have increased production automation, have enabled INKAS\u00ae to provide the fastest turnaround rates in its industry, while adhering to the strict standards that INKAS\u00ae is renowned for.<\/p>\n

US government clients can now more easily purchase a wide range of armored vehicles, including executive sedans and SUVs, tactical or law enforcement vehicles, cash-in-transit vehicles, and more.<\/p>\n

Furthermore, clients can take advantage of an extensive line of defensive technologies and systems to tailor vehicles to their specific needs. Additional upgrades include night-vision systems, sirens and PA systems, electronic surveillance as well as other tactical systems.<\/p>\n
