With the optimized independent rear suspension, this vehicle guarantees a smooth ride and provides the utmost comfort and luxury to its occupants.<\/p>\n

This vehicle brings spaciousness and functionality, and includes an advanced entertainment system with a 36-speaker sound system compatible with Apple\u00a0and Android. Individuals can even choose from a variety of high-quality upholstery and decorative elements to match their personal style.<\/p>\n

The INKAS\u00ae Armored\u00a0Cadillac Escalade is precision-built to exacting standards with innovative, light-weight ballistic armor, covering the vehicle with full, all-around protection. The armoring technologies ensure the vehicle is secured to the ballistic protection level of CEN\u00a01063\u00a0BR6, while maintaining the vehicle\u2019s original features.
