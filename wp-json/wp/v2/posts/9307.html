This is the second time Manorluxx\u00ae<\/sup>\u00a0has been recognized as a winner in the awards history.<\/p>\n

The\u00a0Ontario Export Awards are the province\u2019s most prestigious awards paying tribute to the success and innovative approaches of\u00a0Ontario export companies.\u00a0 Extending across industries and including both rural and urban representation, the awards are a celebration of the contributions exporters have made to both the provincial and national economy.<\/p>\n
