Built to the exacting standards of INKAS\u00ae<\/sup>, the armored Lincoln Navigator L is an all-around luxury vehicle. The vehicle is equipped with a powerhouse twin-turbocharged 3.5L engine, delivering 450hp and 510 lb-ft of torque. Along with this powerful engine, it features six driving modes to deliver performance that matches both your mood and road conditions. In addition to its impressive force, it comes fully equipped with adaptive cruise control, adaptive LED headlamps, enhanced active park assist, a 360-degree lane keeping system, and a pre-collision assist system with automatic breaking.<\/p>\n

Coupled with INKAS\u00ae integrated security solutions, the armored Lincoln Navigator L is one of the most secure SUVs to be driving.<\/p>\n
