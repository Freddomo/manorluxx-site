INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is excited to announce the opening of the 2022 application period for the \u201cRising Star\u201d scholarship program which honours students who show great promise in the fields of Engineering and Business. Two exemplary post-secondary students will be awarded $1,500 CAD each to assist in pursuing their studies.<\/p>\n

As a global leader in its industry, INKAS\u00ae continuously strives to stay at the forefront of innovation, and leverages progressive engineering techniques and state-of-the-art technology in order to deliver cutting-edge security solutions to its clientele worldwide. The selected recipients will encompass INKAS\u00ae values of innovation and leadership, as well as the drive to be the best in their field.<\/p>\n

\u201cThe students of today are the leaders of tomorrow, so it\u2019s essential to help put them on the path to success in their careers,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Chairwoman of INKAS\u00ae and Co-founder of the Khazanski Foundation. \u201cWe believe it\u2019s important to give students the opportunity to excel in their chosen fields and become world-class engineers and business executives.\u201d<\/p>\n

The Khazanski Foundation aims to empower our future leaders as the proud sponsor of the \u201cRising Star\u201d scholarship. Through investing in innovation, raising awareness of critical issues, and supporting individuals, the Foundation strives to make a global impact in order to create a better tomorrow.<\/p>\n

To learn more about the program or to start an application, please visit the \u201cRising Star\u201d scholarship page here<\/a>. The deadline to apply is 11:59PM EDT on March 31, 2022. Recipients of both scholarships will be announced on or near April, 2022.<\/p>\n


\nAbout the INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d<\/strong><\/h3>\n

The INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d is an annual scholarship program, established in 2018 by INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. It recognizes outstanding students who are pursuing or entering full-time studies in business or engineering programs.
