Mr. David Khazanski and Kari Joy Bounma, General Managers of INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is being awarded the ISO Certification Plaque by Mr R.A. Oliver, Senior Partner, Consulting of Business Development Bank of Canada<\/span><\/p>\n

INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce it is now registered to the ISO 9001:2008 standard. The registration was accredited by Orion Assessment Services on December 27th 2010. To become certified as ISO 9001:2008 compliant, INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing underwent a stringent evaluation process, which included the development of a quality management system, a review of management system documentation, initial audit, and finally the implementation audit which identified opportunities for improvement and eliminated non-conformances to the standard.<\/p>\n

The Certificate of compliance with ISO 9001:2008 recognizes that INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing policies, practices and procedures provide for consistent quality of internal processes, product and services we provide to our customers. The Quality Objectives set by the Company demonstrate our commitment to meet customers\u2019 requirements every time all the time.
