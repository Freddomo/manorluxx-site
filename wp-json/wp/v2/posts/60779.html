INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing is proud to announce the recipients of the 2022 \u201cRising Star\u201d scholarship which recognizes exceptional students who are pursuing a post-secondary education in Engineering and Business.<\/p>\n

With an overwhelming number of applicants from across USA and the United States, INKAS is pleased to announce this year\u2019s recipients:<\/p>\n

\u00b7 Sabika Bharmal \u2013 Engineering field of study<\/strong><\/p>\n

\u00b7 Arun Ghag \u2013 Business field of study<\/strong><\/p>\n

\u201cI want to congratulate Sabika and Arun for their achievements, particularly for their initiative to take on leadership roles and pursue their passions,\u201d said Kari Joy Bounma, Co-Founder and Chairwoman of INKAS\u00ae. \u201cBoth of this year\u2019s recipients have overcome great adversity and used their experiences to create positive change in their communities.\u201d<\/p>\n

This year\u2019s recipients show great promise in their future career paths and a commendable dedication to creating a better tomorrow. Sabika is an accomplished woman who is persevering in the male-dominated field of engineering, and pursing her Master\u2019s in Structural Engineering. Arun has overcome societal stereotypes in order to inspire positive change in the world and is passionate about pursuing an education in Business to help create more inclusive workplace environments. INKAS is proud to award the \u201cRising Star\u201d scholarship to support students who show great promise as future leaders in their industries.<\/p>\n

About the INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d<\/strong><\/h3>\n

The INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d is an annual scholarship program, established in 2018 by INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. It recognizes outstanding students who are pursuing or entering full-time studies in business or engineering programs. For those interested in applying for the 2023 scholarship, please make sure to follow INKAS on Twitter and Facebook for further announcements.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
