2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV<\/h2>\n

The 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV<\/a> is more than just a luxury SUV; it’s a fortress on wheels. This armored beauty catches the eye with its bold exterior and offers uncompromised safety for its occupants. There\u2019s a potent 6.2-liter V-8 engine, which generates an impressive 420 horsepower. This is seamlessly paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, and this formidable combination is offered in both traditional rear-wheel drive (RWD) and versatile four-wheel drive (4WD) setups.<\/p>\n

Thanks to innovative lightweight ballistic armor, this vehicle provides all-around protection without sacrificing its original features. The armoring technologies ensure that it meets the stringent CEN 1063 BR6 ballistic protection level while preserving the vehicle’s aesthetics.<\/p>\n

Manorluxx\u00ae has taken security a step further with its proprietary armored overlap system, which safeguards door seals, ensuring that no bullets can penetrate them. Additional protective upgrades include sirens, LED strobe lights, a blackout package, an engine bay fire suppression system, a door-assist mechanism to counteract the weight of armored doors, and much more.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 Toyota Land Cruiser 300<\/h2>\n

The 2023 Toyota Land Cruiser 300 exemplifies the artful balance of supreme comfort, unwavering performance, and off-road prowess. This revamped armored SUV introduces a dash of opulence alongside cutting-edge technology, forging an unrivaled fusion of safety, sophistication, and dynamism.<\/p>\n

The Armored Toyota Land Cruiser (LC300) presents a selection of three formidable engines: a 3.5L V6 petrol engine boasting 400 horsepower, a 4.0L V8 petrol engine delivering 271 horsepower, and a 3.3L V6 diesel engine with an output of 302 horsepower. With a meticulously redesigned powertrain, the vehicle exudes robust power and also demonstrates exceptional off-road agility and handling. It’s also available in market-specific trim levels such as VXR, GXR, GR-Sport, GX, VX, and Sahara ZX for right-hand-drive markets.<\/p>\n

Built to withstand the toughest situations and the most challenging terrains, the Toyota Land Cruiser 300<\/a> ensures 360-degree protection from high-power assault rifles and even the detonation of two DM51 hand grenades. Its armored prowess is certified to the BR6 level, making it a preferred choice for those who need safety without compromise.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 Lexus LX 600<\/h2>\n

The 2023 Lexus LX 600 offers a distinct fusion of luxury and safety. Propelled by a 3.5L V6 engine that boasts an impressive output of 409 horsepower, this vehicle is meticulously crafted to introduce an elevated standard of luxury and off-road capabilities. As a full-size SUV, it firmly establishes its dominance in its category through a harmonious blend of potency, cutting-edge technology, and prestige.<\/p>\n

This impeccably engineered and technologically advanced automobile harnesses the potency of a recently unveiled 3.5L V6 engine, seamlessly integrated with a 10-speed sequential-shift transmission, resulting in an astonishing 409 horsepower that powers it ahead of the competition. Features like all-wheel drive, crawl control, multi-terrain select, and active height control make it both a tech geek’s dream and a trendsetter. Armored by Manorluxx\u00ae, the LX 600 maintains its luxurious features while offering CEN 1063 Level BR6 armoring.<\/p>\n

Manorluxx\u00ae ensures full capsule armoring of the LX 600 to BR6 standards, providing underbody protection against the detonation of two DM51 hand grenades. With 360-degree protection and a host of tactical features available on customer request, the Lexus LX 600<\/a> is an all-around security powerhouse.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 GMC Yukon XL Denali<\/h2>\n

The 2023 GMC Yukon XL Denali<\/a> is not your average luxury SUV; it’s a large, luxurious, and secure vehicle engineered to protect against threats that other vehicles in its class might struggle to withstand. Built on a sturdy truck frame, this armored Yukon Denali is manufactured to withstand the punishment that standard SUVs simply can’t handle.<\/p>\n

Despite its massive size, the Yukon Denali doesn’t compromise on power. Beneath its hood, this vehicle houses a 6.2-liter V8 engine that produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. This robust powerplant offers ample performance to counterbalance the increased weight resulting from the armor.<\/p>\n

Inside the roomy leather-adorned cabin, you’ll discover amenities such as satellite navigation, Bluetooth connectivity, and customizable foot pedals.<\/p>\n

Safety takes precedence, featuring sirens, front and rear LED strobe lights, and a P\/A system designed to discourage potential threats by attracting attention to the situation.<\/p>\n

If tensions ever escalate to gunfire, Manorluxx\u00ae has ensured that the Yukon Denali offers full perimeter armoring capable of stopping up to 308 Winchester FMJ ammunition, in accordance with NIJ Standard 0108.01 Level 3 or CEN 1063 Level BR6.<\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 Chevrolet Tahoe<\/h2>\n

The completely redesigned 2023 Chevrolet Tahoe represents a substantial improvement over its forerunner, boasting an enlarged interior, enhanced ride and handling, and a dominating presence that simply cannot be overlooked.<\/p>\n

This robust SUV blends a spacious and comfortable interior with remarkable performance, off-road aptitude, and towing capacity, rendering it an enticing selection for armor upgrades. Its 6.2-liter V8 engine produces 420 horsepower, coupled with a ten-speed automatic transmission, ensuring it is well-prepared to manage the additional weight of the armor.<\/p>\n

The Manorluxx\u00ae Chevrolet Tahoe comes fully equipped with sirens, front and rear LED strobe lights, and a P\/A system to deter potential threats by attracting attention to the situation. In the event of gunfire, the full perimeter armoring can stop up to 308 Winchester FMJ ammunition, meeting NIJ Standard 0108.01 Level 3 or CEN 1063 Level BR6 standards. If you require a combination of space, security, and style, the Chevrolet Tahoe<\/a> armored by Manorluxx\u00ae is an ideal choice.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 Chevrolet Suburban<\/h2>\n

The 2023 Chevrolet Suburban is an extended version of the Tahoe, offering even more space and comfort. Like its sibling, the Suburban has been transformed into a secure and capable armored vehicle by Manorluxx\u00ae.<\/p>\n

The Armored Chevrolet Suburban SUV<\/a> is an unmistakable choice when it comes to armored vehicles. It combines a large and comfortable interior with impressive performance, off-road capabilities, and towing capacity. The Suburban’s truck-based platform and powerful 5.3L V8 engine producing 355 horsepower make it a perfect candidate for armoring. Inside, it comfortably seats seven occupants, making it ideal for executive transport.<\/p>\n

 <\/p>\n

<\/a><\/h2>\n

2023 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG (aka \u201cG-Wagon\u201d)<\/h2>\n

The Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG, commonly called the “G-Wagon,” remains an emblematic SUV that has garnered the admiration of performance enthusiasts and individuals with a keen focus on security. With 2024 on the horizon, it upholds its renowned status for delivering exceptional performance, agility, and off-road prowess.<\/p>\n

Fitted with a potent AMG 4.0-liter V8 twin-turbo engine, the G-Wagon generates a formidable 577 horsepower, establishing its dominance on the road. Complementing its power is the AMG Performance 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, which provides a front\/rear torque distribution ratio of 40:60, elevating the overall driving experience. However, what truly sets this vehicle apart is its armored configuration.<\/p>\n

Manorluxx\u00ae has armored the G-Wagon to meet CEN 1063 BR6 ballistic standards without compromising its sleek design. Innovative composite materials ensure that the armored G-Wagon looks and drives very similar to its unarmored counterpart. It offers 360-degree protection against high-power rifle ammunition and even the simultaneous detonation of two hand grenades below the vehicle’s floor.<\/p>\n

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG<\/a> has undergone a comprehensive re-engineering of its exterior fascia and interior design. The exterior features a softened and rounded body frame with circular LED High-Performance headlamps and the iconic Mercedes-Benz Panamerica grille. Inside the cabin, you’ll find two large digital screens, Android and Apple connectivity, an adaptive 64-color LED lighting system, and cooled and heated front and rear seats.<\/p>\n

Manorluxx\u00ae ensures the armored G-Wagon is fully equipped with protection for the battery and electronic control module, run-flat devices, high-quality multi-layer bullet-resistant glass, reinforced suspension, and a vehicle security system accessible from the front cabin.<\/p>\n
