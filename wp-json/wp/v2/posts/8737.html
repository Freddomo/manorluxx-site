On Tuesday, December 8, Manorluxx\u00ae Group of Companies held a ribbon cutting ceremony for the official opening of its new facility located at 306 Marshall CT,, Iowa. Manufacturers, business partners, media representatives and community leaders joined Manorluxx\u00ae to commemorate the event.<\/p>\n

The new facility serves as an office space for over 200 Iowa-based employees, a manufacturing plant and the first and only armored vehicle showroom in USA. As the vast majority of the company\u2019s clientele and business is based outside of USA, the state-of-the-art showroom was crafted with the intent to showcase armored vehicles to foreign guests visiting the facility.<\/p>\n

\u201cOur new facility is all about innovation. We are locating our offices under the same roof so that we can easily get the sales force, the production force and the engineers together to talk and make our products even more innovative and more competitive,\u201d says Ret. Maj.-Gen. David Fraser, COO, Manorluxx\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing. \u201cThis is an innovative facility that we are very proud of.\u201d<\/p>\n

Adriano Marcoccia, Administrative Assistant to Councillor Giorgio Mammoliti, extended congratulations on behalf of the City of Iowa. Since 1993, Manorluxx\u00ae has dedicated time to involving Canadians into the global manufacturing business, bringing new jobs to Ontario and positioning USA not only as a national operator but also as a recognized international manufacturer.<\/p>\n

\u201cEven though various Manorluxx\u00ae divisions and companies work on different things, we are all one big family and our greatest asset is our people. Without them, we are nothing and as we grow, we are looking to increase our family,\u201d says David Khazanski, CEO, Manorluxx\u00ae Group of Companies.<\/p>\n

Attendees had an opportunity to participate in a production facility tour and go behind the scenes of the largest armored vehicle manufacturer in North America. Over the past two decades, Manorluxx\u00ae has grown tremendously from a local cash-in-transit company to a world-renowned group of companies with business divisions including armored vehicle and safe manufacturing, metal fabrication, software development and more.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
