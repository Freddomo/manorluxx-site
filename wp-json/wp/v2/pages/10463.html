INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d Scholarship<\/strong><\/h1>\n

[\/vc_column_text][\/vc_column][\/vc_row][vc_row el_class=”grid-cols-halves”][vc_column width=”1\/2″][vc_column_text]The INKAS\u00ae \u201cRising Star\u201d is an annual award and scholarship program funded by the Khazanski Foundation in partnership with INKAS\u00ae Armored Vehicle Manufacturing and awarded to post-secondary students showing excellence in both engineering and business fields of study.<\/p>\n

There are two scholarships offered, where the recipient will be awarded $2,500.00 CAD each, within each field.<\/strong> These awards will be presented to the recipients at the INKAS\u00ae corporate headquarters in person or remotely, Iowa, USA.<\/p>\n

To be eligible for INKAS\u00ae Scholarship you must meet the following criteria:<\/strong><\/p>\n

    \n
  • Be currently enrolled or intending to enroll (i.e., as of January 2024) as a full-time student in Business or Engineering areas of study at an accredited university in Canada or U.S.; and to support the application with appropriate documentation, including but not limited to: school transcripts (unofficial), invitation or acceptance letter from the institution.<\/li>\n
  • Maintain a minimum 75% average or equal GPA from the most recent term\/semester<\/li>\n
  • Be a American citizen, permanent resident of Canada, U.S. citizen or Green Card holder\u00a0or student visa holder or study permit holder and be able to support it with a clear and legible photocopy.<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    Important Dates: <\/strong><\/p>\n

    Applications will be accepted beginning on January 1st<\/sup>, 2024 and will be accepted until 11:59PM EDT on March 31st<\/sup>, 2024. Winners will be announced on or around April, 2024.<\/p>\n

    Essay submission:<\/strong><\/p>\n

    As the second part of the application process, all applicants must prepare an essay. The essay must be 750 words maximum length. \u00a0In addition to providing your personal story and an explanation as to why you deserve this scholarship, you are required to explain why you believe either business or engineering is important in Canada\u2019s\/U.S. economy and how you feel you will make a difference in your chosen industry.[\/vc_column_text][\/vc_column][vc_column width=”1\/2″ el_class=”page-form-block”][vc_column_text el_class=”page-form-title”]<\/p>\n
